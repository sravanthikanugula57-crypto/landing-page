Our Story

A Tradition Born in a Family Kitchen

Spice Garden began as a humble dhaba on the outskirts of Hyderabad in 1998. What started with three recipes and a clay oven has grown into a beloved dining destination.

Every ingredient is sourced fresh from local farms. Every spice is ground in-house. Every recipe is a secret passed down through generations.
